Expertise

Unlock the full potential of your cloud journey with my expertise in transforming, automating, and optimizing infrastructure. With a strong foundation in architecture design, cost efficiency, and technical mentorship, I bring solutions that drive innovation and ensure reliability.

Cloud Architecture Design

Design scalable, secure, and cost-efficient solutions on AWS, GCP, and Azure. From high- availability systems to hybrid-cloud strategies, I align architecture with business goals.

DevOps & Automation

Build CI/CD pipelines, automate deployments with Jenkins/GitLab, and orchestrate Kubernetes clusters. Focus on accelerating releases while ensuring reliability.

Site Reliability Engineering (SRE)

mplement monitoring (ELK, CloudWatch), automate incident response, and optimize performance for 99.99% uptime. Turn infrastructure into a self-healing asset.

Cloud Migration & Modernization

Migrate legacy apps to cloud-native environments (EKS/GKE) with zero downtime. Containerize workloads and adopt microservices for future-proof scalability.

Infrastructure as Code (IaC)

Automate cloud provisioning using Terraform and CloudFormation. Ensure consistency, version control, and auditability across multi-cloud environments.

Cloud Cost Optimization

Audit spending, rightsize resources, and implement auto-scaling policies. Slash cloud bills without compromising performance.

Security & Compliance

Harden cloud environments with IAM policies, encryption, and tools like Vault. Align with GDPR, HIPAA, and industry-specific frameworks.

Technical Mentorship

Train teams on DevOps practices, IaC, and SRE principles. Upskill engineers through hands-on workshops and real-world scenario coaching.
